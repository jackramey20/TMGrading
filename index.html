What We Do

Our Services

From rough grading to finished asphalt, JM Grading handles every phase of your project with precision and care.

◾

Land Grading

We level and shape land for residential, commercial, and agricultural projects — ensuring proper drainage and a stable foundation for construction.

◾

Asphalt Paving

Driveways, parking lots, roadways, and more. We install long-lasting asphalt surfaces using quality materials and proven techniques.

◾

Excavation

Precision excavation for foundations, utilities, drainage systems, and site development on properties of any size.

◾

Site Preparation

Complete site clearing, grubbing, and preparation services to get your land build-ready on schedule and within budget.

◾

Drainage Solutions

Properly engineered drainage systems including swales, retention basins, and culverts to protect your property from water damage.

◾

Compaction & Sub-Base

Thorough soil compaction and sub-base installation to ensure the structural integrity of your pavement or building site.

About JM Grading

Built on Trust & Hard Work

JM Grading was founded with a simple mission: deliver high-quality grading and paving work that stands the test of time. Based in Visalia, California, we proudly serve the entire San Joaquin Valley — from residential homeowners to large commercial developers.

Our team brings decades of combined experience to every job site. We invest in well-maintained equipment and employ skilled operators who take pride in precise, efficient work. When you hire JM Grading, you get a contractor who shows up on time, communicates clearly, and delivers results you can see and stand on.
